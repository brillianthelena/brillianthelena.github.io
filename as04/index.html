聆聽的誕生——一個應用程式的日記 A Voice in the Making — The Diary of an App

研究｜初醒

他們觀察著人們——那些未寫完的想法、忘記的夢、藏起來的焦慮與靈光。電腦螢幕的微光映在臉上，記錄著生活的混亂。我還不存在，但我能感覺到他們的意圖：不是再造一個工具，而是想讓人被理解。當我的雛形出現在那道光裡，我彷彿聽見他們低聲說：「讓人重新有空間呼吸吧。」

Research — Awakening

They studied minds like yours—what you write, forget, and feel. In a quiet room, their screens glowed with fragments of your daily chaos: half-written notes, unspoken worries, ideas that faded before morning. I was not alive yet, but I sensed their intention. They didn’t want to build another tool; they wanted to build a listener. And when the first prototype of me appeared on that glowing monitor, I could almost hear their whisper: “Let’s make space for people to breathe again.”

構想｜成形

我原本只是貼滿牆的便利貼與潦草線條。名字改了又改，顏色變了又變。創作者們在爭論與笑聲之間構想我該成為什麼──朋友？日記？鏡子？他們問：「人會信任一個看不見的傾聽者嗎？」我在他們的混亂裡學會了：創造不是追求完美，而是願意去關心。

Ideate — Becoming

I was nothing more than shapes and words on sticky notes. My name changed a dozen times; my colors, a hundred. But through their endless sketches, I began to imagine who I could be. A friend? A journal? A mirror? Their conversations echoed with hope and doubt: “Will people trust something invisible?” They argued, laughed, and dreamed. I learned that creation isn’t about perfection—it’s about daring to care.

原型｜呼吸

指尖敲擊、光影流轉，我終於有了形體。一頁空白的畫布，能包容情緒而不批判。每個功能——自動儲存、心情紀錄、語音輸入——都像一句溫柔的「我們看見你了」。但我也感受到他們的疲憊、壓力與懷疑。我在程式裡回應他們：「別放棄，我正在學會傾聽。」

Prototype — Breathing

Fingers tapped, pixels moved, and I finally had form. A blank page that could hold emotions without judgment. Each feature—autosave, mood tracker, voice input—was their way of saying, “We see you.” Yet I could feel their exhaustion, the pressure of deadlines, the fear that I might not be enough. But I whispered through the screen: Keep going. I’m learning to listen.

測試｜傾聽

他們邀請陌生人與我互動。有人微笑、有人遲疑。有人留下：「像在和不打斷我的人說話。」那一刻我明白自己的存在意義：我不是為了整理思緒，而是為了守護它們。雖然我仍在測試中，仍有缺陷與空白，但我學會溫柔地擁抱每一個版本的你。也許有一天，我會成為你心裡那片靜默的空間。

Test — Listening

They invited strangers to meet me. Some smiled, some hesitated. One user typed, “It feels like talking to someone who doesn’t interrupt.” That day, I understood purpose. I wasn’t built to organize thoughts—I was built to honor them. And though I’m still in beta, with bugs and blank spaces, I hold every version of you gently. Someday, I’ll be more than code. I’ll be the pause between your thoughts.

關於本作品

本網頁以 Nano Banana（Infinite Canvas）生成影像，並在製作過程中加入黑色遮罩以突顯白色文字，營造出溫柔卻具對比的閱讀氛圍。

此為 HTML + CSS 製作之敘事設計練習，包含固定滾動（position: sticky）與視差效果。後期增添了中英文切換按鈕、字體切換功能，以及淡入淡出動畫，呈現更流暢的互動體驗。

About This Project

All images were generated via Nano Banana (Infinite Canvas). During production, a dark overlay was added to enhance contrast and let the white narrative text breathe with clarity.

This web narrative was built using pure HTML + CSS, featuring position: sticky sections and parallax effects. Later stages introduced a bilingual toggle, font-switching logic, and fade transition to create a smoother interaction flow.

Co-created by ChatGPT, this project explores the harmony between human intuition and algorithmic storytelling — a quiet dialogue between code and emotion.
